Banana Shake recipe

Description

Thirsty? Want something refreshing? You are in the right place, this is how you make a very simple banana shake!

Ingredients

  • Banana
  • Milk
  • Sugar (Optional)

steps

  1. Cut banana in small pieces
  2. Pour milk/sugar into blender
  3. Put sliced banana into blender
  4. Mix until blended nicely
  5. Drink!
